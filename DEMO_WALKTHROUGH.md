# College ERP - Demo Walkthrough Guide

This guide details the step-by-step flow to demonstrate the "College ERP System".

## 1. Admin Workflow
**Login**: `admin@college.edu` / `admin123`

1.  **Dashboard**:
    -   Show the KPI cards (Total Students, Staff, Fees).
    -   Show the Fee Collection Line Chart and Student Distribution Pie Chart.
2.  **Student Management**:
    -   Go to **Students** menu.
    -   Show the DataGrid with search and sort.
    -   Highlight the "Status" chips and Action buttons.
3.  **Fee Management**:
    -   Go to **Fees** menu.
    -   Show the Fee Dashboard (Total Demand vs Collected).
    -   Scroll down to the Recent Fee Records table.

## 2. Teacher Workflow
**Login**: `smith@college.edu` / `teacher123`

1.  **Dashboard (Mobile View)**:
    -   Switch browser to Mobile View (iPhone 12/14).
    -   Show the "Classes Today" and "Leaves Taken" cards.
2.  **Attendance**:
    -   Tap **Attendance** in the bottom nav.
    -   Select a Class.
    -   Show the **Roll Number Grid**.
    -   Tap a few numbers to toggle status (Green -> Red -> Orange).
    -   Toggle "Edit Mode".
3.  **Leave Application**:
    -   Tap **Leave** in the bottom nav.
    -   Show the history cards.
    -   Tap the **Floating Action Button (+)**.
    -   Fill the modal to apply for leave.

## 3. Student Workflow
**Login**: `cs001@student.college.edu` / `student123`

1.  **Dashboard (Mobile View)**:
    -   Show the large **Attendance Pie Chart** (Green/Red).
    -   Show the "Fee Due" alert card.
2.  **My Fees**:
    -   Tap **Fees** in the bottom nav.
    -   Show the fee cards with Green (Paid) and Red (Pending) borders.
3.  **Timetable**:
    -   Tap **Timetable**.
    -   Show the vertical timeline view of classes.
